The program

The National Science Foundationâ€™s SBIR (Small Business Innovation Research) program exists to promote scientific excellence and technological innovation nationwide. It does this by providing no-equity funding to small businesses so they can conduct research on innovative, socially beneficial products and services. To identify the best and most potentially impactful advances, the SBIR program accepts proposal from nearly all areas of technology.

Successful grant applicants will receive funding up to $225,000 to complete a phase-one (proof-of-concept) project within twelve months. Phase-two projects may be eligible for grants of up to $750,000. To find out whether your business is eligible for funding, visit our Apply page now.
